It's been a long seven years, but the moment has finally arrived. As launch day is fast-approaching, Tamriel Foundry decided to take a trip down memory lane.  From the creation of ZeniMax Media to the release of the pre-order bonuses - with the promise of much more to come - check out some of the biggest announcements in The Elder Scrolls Online history!

2007

  • August 1st, 2007 - Officials from ZeniMax Media, the parent company of Elder Scrolls developer Bethesda, have announced the founding of ZeniMax Online Studios, a new division focused on developing new massively multiplayer online games. The company will be headed by MMO veteran Matt Firor, one of the founders of the now EA-owned Mythic Entertainment. - GamaSutra

2012

  • May 3rd, 2012 - Game Informer officially announces The Elder Scrolls Online which is developed by the team at Zenimax Online Studios. "The Elder Scrolls Online merges the unmatched exploration of rich worlds that the franchise is known for with the scale and social aspects of a massively multiplayer online role-playing game". - Game Informer
  • May 7th, 2012 - Game Informer's Adam Biessener sits down with Creative Director Paul Sage for the first time to discuss The Elder Scrolls Online. Paul Sage puts our mind at ease in the 19 minute video interview discussing the goals for the companies first MMO title. - Game Informer
  • May 9th, 2012 - Paul Sage explains how the world of The Elder Scrolls Online came to be in this article in Game Informer. Everything from getting a team together to work on the MMO to why the game takes place in the Second Era is explained in the 6 minute video interview. - Game Informer
  • May 25th, 2012 - Game Informer gets a deep understanding of why Zenimax Online Studios isn't using the HeroEngine. Matt Firor answers a few interesting questions to give us insight into this decision. - Game Informer
  • October 15th, 2012 - Zenimax Online Studios announces the re-launch of elderscrollsonline.com. In this re-launch they introduce a number of new features including an official Facebook and Twitter page. - Official Elder Scrolls Online
